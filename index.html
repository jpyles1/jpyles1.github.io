What is ECPA?

The electronics Communication Privacy Act of 1986 was passed to expand and update federal laws against wiretapping and electronic eavesdropping.
The ECPA includes amendments to the wiretap act and created the stored communications act and pen register act. The wiretap act concerned the
interception of electronic and wire communication.
